About The Project


Our idea to help the children getting heard, is to send a representative from Redd Barna to villages collecting different opinions, videos, and quotes weekly. This information will be posted on this webpage dedicated to share the children’s opinions about climate change. To promote the webpage, we have taken inspiration from the marketing techniques of Fyre Festival. This consists of creating a hype around a QR-code with a countdown. Fyre festival had celebrities and influencers share a blank, bright orange photo without any other information to it, mystifying the whole project. This made a lot of people question the photo and what the reason behind the photo was, causing a major viral hype. The reason why we have chosen to use this advertisement, is to get a lot of people talking about “what’s going to happen when the timer reaches zero?”.
As stated above, we want to get the unheard voices of the children out to the public. We belive that in this way, if we can hear what the smallest victims are experiencing every day, we can make a difference! They also have great insight and new takes on the matter that we haven't even considered. If you feel moved by their stories, and want to help them, you can do that by donating to this cause directly here on this site. Either way, there is much you can learn simply by reading their powerful stories!
